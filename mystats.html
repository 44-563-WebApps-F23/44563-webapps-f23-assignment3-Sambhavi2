Dosa

I like dosa for its crispy texture, delightful taste, and versatility. It's a simple yet flavorful dish made from fermented rice and lentils. With various fillings and sides, it offers a satisfying and delicious meal. Easy on the stomach and energizing, dosa is a favorite for breakfast or dinner in Indian cuisine.
